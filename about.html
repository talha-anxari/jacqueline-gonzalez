About the Author

Jacqueline Gonzalez

Ridge & Coast Writer

Jacqueline Carol Lytle Gonzalez is a passionate storyteller and dedicated nurse with over 40 years of experience. Born and raised on an apple farm in North Carolina, she draws from her rich heritage to share her life's journey in "From the Ridge to the Water." Jacqueline's diverse background includes leadership roles in nursing, where she has touched countless lives through her care and compassion. She believes in the power of sharing stories to inspire others and foster connection. As a loving mother and wife, Jacqueline

"I write to find my way back to the ridge, even when I'm standing by the sea."

About the Book

Some distances aren't measured in miles.

"From the Ridge to the Water is a heartfelt memoir by Jacqueline Carol Lytle Gonzalez that takes readers on a journey through her life, filled with love, resilience, and the beauty of nature. Growing up on a rural apple farm in North Carolina, Jacqueline shares stories of her family's struggles and triumphs, highlighting the values of hard work and kindness instilled in her by her parents. The book reflects on significant life changes, the importance of community, and the lessons learned from both joy and hardship. Through vivid storytelling, Jacqueline invites readers to explore her roots and discover how her experiences shaped her into the person she is today. This inspiring narrative not only celebrates family bonds but also emphasizes the power of hope and perseverance in overcoming life's challenges.

— Jacqueline Gonzalez
